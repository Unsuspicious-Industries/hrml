Unsuspicious Industries operates at the edge of defensible autonomy, and we need an Ethics Researcher who can translate values into enforceable mechanisms. Responsibilities

  • Develop evaluative frameworks for new experiments, deployments, and datasets.
  • You convert ethical judgements into typing rules
  • Check if we can at all time will that our logical frameworks should become a universal law.
  • Track AI developpement, study existentil risk and defend our line on LessWrong in the comment section of Yudkowsky’s posts.

What helps

  • 4+ years in applied ethics, safety research, or adjacent risk roles, but a big hatred for the Ai safety folks.
  • Working knowledge of technical ML systems and their failure modes.
  • Published research, or at least twitter threads with more than 50 likes.
  • Knowing around 10 words in at least 5 other language besides English is a plus.
