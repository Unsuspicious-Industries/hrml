About Unsuspicious

Unsuspicious Industries

Founded in 2023, Unsuspicious Industries is an interdisciplinary research institution dedicated to advancing human knowledge through rigorous scientific inquiry. Our faculty and research staff pursue fundamental questions across computational sciences, mathematics, artificial intelligence, and related fields: guided by doubt, curiosity, and ADHD.
