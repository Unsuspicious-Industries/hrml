Ethics Policy

Unsuspicious Industries is committed to upholding the latest discoveries in ethics and ensuring that all our practices align with the most recent technology in ethics research.

Our Ethics research Division

In order to maximize our moral value, we have established a dedicated Ethics research division. The goal of this division is to discover new moral laws and ensure our practices align with their findings. We employ the most advanced technology, including cutting-edge philosophy, AI-assisted reasoning, polls, twitter threads, and more, to ensure our ethical standards are always up-to-date.

Ethical Decision-Making

We encourage a culture of ethical decision-making, where employees can launch debates on ethical dilemmas and explore new moral paths.

Reporting Violations

If you believe that our ethical standards have been compromised, or aren't up to date, we encourage you to report your concerns to us. We are committed to investigating all reports promptly and taking appropriate action.

Continuous Improvement

We are dedicated to continuously reviewing and improving our ethics policy and practices to ensure they remain relevant and effective. We actively try to get out of the hegemonic frameworks of traditional ethics by incorporating diverse perspectives and methodologies.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our Ethics Policy, please contact us at contact@unsuspicious.org.
